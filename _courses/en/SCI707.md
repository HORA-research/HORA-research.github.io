## Course Overview

The Interdisciplinary Summer School on Digital Health [(EINS)](https://eins.griis.ca) brings together experts from various disciplines to introduce participants to the challenges of implementing a learning healthcare system (LHS).

The summer school will enable you to:

1. Identify and understand the challenges involved in the various stages of implementing an LHS; understand the challenges and importance of structuring knowledge and data within an LHS;
2. Recognize the scope of various data analysis methods and identify analytical biases within the LHS cycle;
3. Identify the ethical, legal, and social acceptability issues related to the reuse of health data in an LHS;
4. Work in an interdisciplinary environment while contributing specialized expertise on a specific aspect of the LHS cycle.
